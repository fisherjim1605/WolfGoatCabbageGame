WOLF, GOAT & CABBAGE GAME



LABELED STATE TRANSITIONS

We can also use an LST to solve the Wolf, Goat & Cabbage puzzle:

  • The states will be whatever side of the river the animals, cabbage and boat are on
  • The transition (action) will be what is crossing the river at that time

On the right is a section from the LST of the game:

  • We start in State 0 (S0 in the diagram)
  • Moving the wolf puts us in State 3 (S3)
  • S3 is an End State because the goat then eats the cabbage and no more moves can be made
  • Looking back to S0, moving the goat puts us in State 4 (S4)
  • From S4, we can move the goat back to the other side to go back to S0
