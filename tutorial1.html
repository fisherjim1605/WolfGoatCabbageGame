WOLF, GOAT & CABBAGE GAME



CONTROLS

Move the animals and boat to the other side of the river by clicking them

You can only move the animals if the boat is on the same side

The aim of the game is to get all the animals from the left side of the river to the right

If the wolf is alone with the goat, the wolf will eat it

If the goat is alone with the cabbage, the goat will eat it

If the wolf or the goat eats something, then it's game over
