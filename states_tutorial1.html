LABELED STATE TRANSITIONS

Labeled State Transitions (or LSTs for short) are diagrams that show how a system moves through different stages or states

For example, an LTS could be used to show the stages of a game of catch:

  • There are two states in a game of catch: player 1 has the ball, and player 2 has the ball
  • The transition (action) that changes the state of the game is the act of one player throwing the ball to the other
